Current Data Posture

No account, no ads, no third-party ad tracking. The app uses local saves, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and one optional StoreKit Full Adventure unlock.

About The Game

Pip is a small apprentice keeper caught inside a tower shuffle. Every room is a readable little tactical puzzle: pick a route, grab the right tile, beat the objective, and step through the exit. Boss rooms raise the stakes with tower-specific tricks like clones, lane attacks, jumping threats, and danger zones.

Fair And Fast

Runs are built around short rooms, visible consequences, and power-ups that make old tiles feel new without asking players to log in or grind online systems.

Bosses Fight Back

Each tower boss changes the puzzle: some chase Pip, some split into copies, some mark the floor, and some punish greedy routes.

Fair Free Start

The first five towers and basic Daily Challenge are free. Full Adventure is a one-time unlock for towers 6-10, Hard Daily, and the ending.

Built For iPhone

The game is portrait-first, one-thumb friendly, and includes sound, haptics, reduced motion, and high-contrast settings.
