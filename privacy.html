Privacy Policy

Privacy for Pip vs 10 Towers.

Effective date: May 6, 2026. This policy explains what the iPhone game collects, what stays on your device, and how to contact support.

Short Version

Pip vs 10 Towers does not require an account, does not sell personal data, and does not use data for third-party advertising tracking. The game stores progress, settings, and the Full Adventure unlock locally. It also uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand gameplay balance, reliability, purchases, and crashes.

No Account

The game does not ask for your name, email address, password, contacts, photos, or precise location.

No Ads

The current release does not include third-party advertising SDKs, ad personalization, or cross-app advertising tracking.

Local Progress

Best scores, completed towers, settings, and similar gameplay state are stored on your device.

Optional Purchase

Full Adventure is a one-time StoreKit purchase. Apple processes payment; the app stores only the unlock status.

Services We Use

Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to log game events such as tower selection, room completion, item collection, boss pressure, upgrade selection, purchase prompts, and settings changes. These events help improve pacing, clarity, balance, and purchase flow reliability.

Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to receive crash reports and limited game-state breadcrumbs so crashes can be fixed faster.

Firebase is provided by Google. Google may process device and app information as part of Firebase services. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

What We Do Not Collect

No Account Data

No account, username, password, profile, or player account database is required for the current release.

No Sensitive Device Content

The app does not access contacts, photos, camera, microphone, calendar, messages, or precise GPS location.

No Payment Cards

The app does not collect payment card numbers or billing details. Full Adventure purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit.

Your Choices

Local Data

You can remove local game progress and settings by deleting the app from your device. Because there is no account system, support cannot recover deleted local saves.

Support Data

You may request deletion of support emails or attachments you sent by contacting pondd.me@gmail.com.

Analytics And Crash Data

Analytics and crash data are used only to improve the game and reliability. They are not used for third-party advertising tracking.

Children's Privacy

The app is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child sent personal information through support, contact us and we will remove it.

Contact And Changes

For privacy questions, email pondd.me@gmail.com. We may update this policy when the app changes, especially if future versions add accounts, cloud saves, purchases, ads, or new third-party services. The effective date at the top will change when the policy changes.
